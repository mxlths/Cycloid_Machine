# Hardware Components

This document outlines the hardware components required for building the Cycloid Machine, based on the project's Bill of Materials (BOM).

## Main Assemblies

The machine consists of four primary assemblies:

1. Drive Assembly
2. Frame Assembly
3. Platen Assembly
4. Rod Arm Assembly

### Drive Assembly

The Drive Assembly contains three identical sub-assemblies, each with the following components:

#### Servo Mount (x3)
- 4 × M3 screws
- 1 × Servo Mount
- 4 × M5 screws
- 4 × M5 2020 nuts
- 1 × 20T GT2 pulley
- 1 × Drive Belt

#### Drive Wheel (x3)
- 1 × Drive Wheel Mount (3D printed)
- 1 × 60T GT2 pulley
- 4 × M4 bolts (for bearing to wheel mount)
- 4 × M4 nuts (for bearing to wheel mount)
- 1 × 75mm Lazy Susan Bearing
- 1 × Bearing to Pulley Mount (3D printed)
- 4 × M5 bolts (for drive wheel mount to 2040 rail)
- 4 × M5 2020 nuts (for drive wheel mount to 2040 rail)
- 2 × M3 40mm screws (locks pulley to pulley mount)

#### Drive Wheel Assembly (x3)
- 1 × Rod to Bearing Mount (3D printed)
- 1 × Bearing
- 1 × Drive Wheel (3D printed)
- 4 × M8 nuts (captive nuts)
- 1 × M8 25mm cap head bolt (bearing mount bolt)
- 1 × M5 60mm bolt (bolts drive wheel to drive wheel mount)
- 1 × M5 nylock nut (for drive wheel to drive wheel mount)

### Frame Assembly

The Frame Assembly provides structural support for the machine:

- 2 × 2040 aluminum extrusion rails
- 1 × 2060 aluminum extrusion rail
- 2 × 2020 aluminum extrusion rails
- Connectors to suit

### Platen Assembly

The Platen Assembly consists of two main sub-assemblies:

#### Platen to Rail Mount
- 1 × 2060 to Bearing Mount
- 4 × M4 bolts
- 4 × M4 nuts
- 4 × M6 bolts
- 4 × M6 nuts
- 1 × 100mm Lazy Susan Bearing
- 1 × 60T GT2 pulley
- 1 × Platen (500mm circle or square)

#### Servo Mount
- 4 × M3 screws
- 1 × Servo Mount
- 4 × M5 screws
- 4 × M5 2020 nuts
- 1 × 20T GT2 pulley
- 1 × Drive Belt

### Rod Arm Assembly

The Rod Arm Assembly includes the components that connect the wheels to the pen:

#### Rod to Wheel Plate
- Rod to Rod components

#### Pen Holder Assembly
- 1 × M4 40mm hex bolt (locks in pen)
- 1 × M4 nut (captive nut)
- 1 × Nut Knob (3D printed)
- 1 × Pen Holder Assembly (3D printed, parallel or end style)
- 1 × M3 30mm bolt (connects pen holder assembly to rod)
- 1 × M3 nut (connects pen holder assembly to rod)

## Critical Dimensions

From the CAD TO-DO file, some critical dimensions include:

- Bearing: 22mm OD, 8mm ID, 7mm Depth
- Aluminum Rod: Holes at 8.4mm for mild friction joint

## Design Considerations

There are several design elements currently under consideration:

1. Offset multiple pen holder design for creating multiple patterns simultaneously
2. Improved bearing retention mechanisms for rod connectors
